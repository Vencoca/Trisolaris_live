Challenging today’s conventional operations in the energy markets & ushering in the future of trading.

Drawing on sophisticated mathematical models and leading-edge technologies, TRISOLARIS transforms data into top-tier trading across Europe and globally. Our trading portfolio includes electricity, gas and renewable energy products.

TRISOLARIS offers market-beating energy portfolio management services. As a Balance Responsible Party, our team handles buying and selling electricity on electricity exchanges, as well as managing balance responsibility. TRISOLARIS also facilitates the construction of renewable energy parks and provides power purchase agreements to enable the establishment of new parks.
